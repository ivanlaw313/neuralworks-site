Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026  ·  App: DestinyAI — 命盤・AI靈犀  ·  Developer: Neuralworks

1. Data We Collect

DestinyAI stores your birth date, birth time, birth place, and name locally on your device only, using Apple's on-device storage. This personal data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

2. Advertising (Free Version)

The free version of DestinyAI displays advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) and device information to serve personalised ads, subject to your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent choice.

You may opt out at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. If you deny tracking, contextual ads will be shown instead.

For more information on how Google uses data: Google Privacy Policy.

3. Analytics

We do not collect any analytics, crash reports, or usage data beyond what Apple provides to all App Store developers through App Store Connect aggregated metrics.

4. In-App Purchases

The "Remove Ads" in-app purchase is processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive, store, or process any payment information.

5. Children's Privacy

DestinyAI is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

6. Data Security

All user data is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS system-level encryption. We have no access to this data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
