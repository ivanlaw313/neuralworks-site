Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my BaZi chart? On first launch, accept the disclaimer, then enter your birth date, time, gender, and name. Tap "Start" to generate your chart. You can add more charts later from the Settings tab.
The birth time is unknown — what do I enter? If you don't know your exact birth time, choose noon (12:00) as a neutral default. The hour pillar will be less accurate, but the other three pillars will be correct.
How does the daily fortune score work? The score (0–100) is calculated by comparing today's day pillar (干支) with your personal chart's Five Elements balance. A higher score means today's elements are more favorable for your chart.
How do I use the Moon Block oracle (擲筊)? Go to the Oracle tab. Shake your device or tap the "Cast" button. The blocks will fall and show one of three results: Holy (聖筊), Yin (陰筊), or Laughing (笑筊), each with a detailed interpretation.
How do I change the language? Go to Settings → Language and select your preferred language. The app will switch immediately without restarting.
How do I remove ads? Go to Settings → Remove Ads → tap the purchase button. It's a one-time payment of $1.99. You can restore your purchase on a new device using "Restore Purchase" in Settings.
My "Remove Ads" purchase isn't restoring — what do I do? Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. If the issue persists, email us with your Apple ID (email only, not password) and we'll investigate.
