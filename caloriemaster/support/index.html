Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a meal? Open the Diary tab, tap the + button, and search for a food or scan a barcode. Select the portion size and confirm. Your calorie and macro totals update automatically.
How is my daily calorie target calculated? We use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to estimate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), then adjust it based on your activity level and weight goal. You can customize this in Settings → Goals.
Where does the BMI formula come from? We use the standard Body Mass Index formula defined by the World Health Organization (WHO): BMI = weight (kg) / height (m)². Learn more at WHO — A Healthy Lifestyle.
Are the diet and nutrition recommendations reliable? Our recommendations are based on published guidelines from the U.S. Dietary Guidelines (USDA) and the WHO Healthy Diet fact sheet. CalorieMaster is intended for general wellness tracking, not medical advice — always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making significant dietary changes.
How do I scan a food barcode? In the Diary tab, tap the + button, then tap the barcode icon. Point your camera at the barcode. You may need to grant camera permission on first use.
What is included in Pro? Pro unlocks unlimited meal history, advanced macro breakdowns, custom food creation, export to CSV, and removes ads. Pro is available as a monthly or yearly subscription.
How do I manage or cancel my subscription? All subscriptions are billed through Apple. To manage or cancel, open the iOS Settings app → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → select CalorieMaster Pro.
How do I restore a purchase on a new device? Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. If the issue persists, email us with your Apple ID (email only, not password).
How do I delete my account and data? Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, meal history, and preferences. You can also email us to request deletion.
How do I change the language? CalorieMaster follows your iOS system language automatically. Change your device language in iOS Settings → General → Language & Region.
