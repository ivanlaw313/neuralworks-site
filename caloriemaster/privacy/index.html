Privacy Policy — CalorieMaster

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Neuralworks ("we", "us") built CalorieMaster to help you track nutrition and wellness. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects and how we use it.

Data Stored On Your Device

The following information stays on your device and is not sent to our servers:

  • Profile information (name, age, sex, height, weight, goals)
  • Meal and food log entries
  • Calorie and macro totals
  • App preferences and settings

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your personal data for targeted advertising.

Third-Party Services

CalorieMaster may display non-personalized advertisements via Google AdMob. These ads do not use the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) for tracking. For more information, see the Google AdMob Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Subscription and purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive your payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

CalorieMaster is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Rights

Because your data is stored on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or tapping Settings → Account → Delete Account within the app.
